Other Details

Objectives in the Robotics Club

  • To further develop skills such as Programming, Coding, and Designing.
  • To gain more experience in activities in the technological field.
  • To learn about the significance of technology in our generation.

Requirements for Club Application

  1. 90 or above grade in the ICT subject
  2. 85 or above grades in report card
  3. No current membership in other non-academic clubs
  4. ICT Qualifying Exam (details TBA)

Activities for SY 25-26

Robotics Fair
- An end-of-year experience where students will show their finished robotics project to the rest of the school

Inter-Campus Robotics Competition
- A competition where the robotics team of each campus will showcase their programming skills in developing applications.
